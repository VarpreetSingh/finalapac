Asia-Pacific Institute of Management publishes an international quarterly refereed journal (Bi-annual till 2007) "Asia-Pacific Business Review (ABR)" now renamed as "Asia-Pacific Journal of Management Research and Innovation", (ISSN: 2319-510X & RNI: DELENG/2012/46197) Published and marketed globally through SAGE Publications Pvt. Ltd.

The journal aims at disseminating new knowledge in the field of management and information technology and provides a forum for deliberations and exchange of knowledge among academics, industries, researchers. The Journal encourages submission of theoretical and empirical papers, case studies, research notes, executive experience sharing and review articles in the different domains of management and Information Technology and related disciplines. Book reviews and commentaries on recent business updates are also considered for publication. All work submitted to the journal go through a double blind reviewing process as per the standard procedures of well known refereed journals. However the review process could be as fast as a fortnight in some typical cases. Empirical research work on current analysis is published on priority.

Manuscript Submission
Manuscripts for consideration of publication must be prepared in accordance with the (APA) Guidelines to authors. Two copies of the manuscript should be submitted along with the Contributor's Agreement Form designated by SAGE

Reviewers Invited
Senior Researchers in the different domains of management and information technology are invited for the reviewers panel of the journal. Interested scholars may communicate the interest to the editor with a brief profile. Nominations are also invited for the editorial advisory board of the journal.
